Community

The Cystic Fibrosis Foundation recognizes the value of tapping into the expertise that only people with CF and their families have. We invite them to share their insights to help improve and develop programs and services that support them in their daily lives.

What You Need to Know

CF Adult and Family Advisors

The CF Adult and Family Advisors (AFA) group is composed of people with cystic fibrosis, parents, caregivers, spouses and siblings who voluntarily provide input on matters related to CF care, research and quality of life. Learn More

Contribute to Our Research

We are asking the cystic fibrosis community to help drive CF research. Insight CF gathers your questions and uses data in the Patient Registry to answer them. Submit your question by Jan. 31, 2017. Learn More

Impact Grants

Impact Grants provide up to $10,000 per year to individuals or organizations that benefit the cystic fibrosis community. Learn More
